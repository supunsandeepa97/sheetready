Questions

Good to know before you download

Here are the most common questions about SheetReady's spreadsheet templates — Excel and Google Sheets compatibility, how instant delivery works through Gumroad, the refund policy, and whether you need any accounting knowledge (you don't).

Do these work in both Excel and Google Sheets?+

Yes. Every template is built to open in Microsoft Excel and to import into Google Sheets. You can use whichever you already have — no special software, plugins or add-ons are required to start tracking.

How do I receive my template after buying?+

Checkout and delivery run through Gumroad. The moment your payment clears you get an instant download link on screen and by email, so you can open your spreadsheet right away — there's no waiting and nothing is shipped.

Do I need any accounting knowledge to use them?+

No. The formulas, categories and dashboards are already built for you. You simply type your numbers into the labelled cells and the totals, charts and summaries update on their own. If you can fill in a cell, you can use it.

Is the Simple Monthly Money Tracker really free?+

Yes, completely free. Add it on Gumroad at no cost and download it instantly. It's the best way to try the SheetReady style before buying one of the paid business or budgeting templates.

What's your refund policy?+

If a paid template isn't what you expected, reply to your Gumroad receipt within 30 days and we'll sort out a refund. Because these are instant digital downloads, we appreciate you trying the free tracker first.

Can I use one template for more than one business?+

Yes. Once you've downloaded a template it's yours to copy and reuse across your own projects, businesses or clients. Please don't resell or redistribute the files themselves — the templates stay with you.
